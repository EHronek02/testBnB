Our Story

After years of city living, we found this 1920s farmhouse nestled in the mountains and knew it was meant to be shared. What began as a personal retreat soon became a passion project to create a welcoming space for others to unwind and reconnect with nature.

Every corner of Gretchensville reflects our love for sustainable living, local craftsmanship, and the simple pleasures of good food, good company, and breathtaking views.
