Our Story

What does it take to build this state of the art home in this remote town of Siaya? It’s the love in our hearts and the love for our town.

That love is what we’ve now decided to extend to our dear clients who visit Siaya and its proximity, looking for a place to spend a night or two.

In that love, you will find peace and joy in our elegant enclosed and secured parameters, immaculate cozy rooms, and exemplary customer service.

Whether you’re on a holiday with family, working on a project along the shores of Victoria or you and your friends are simply escaping a busy city life to take it easy somewhere secluded, you all have a place at Gretchensville. Our pledge is to do our best to make you feel safe, comfortable and valued.

Our home is one of a kind in Siaya. Be it you’re visiting from Spokane WA, Juja, Kiambu or you’re just a neighbor within, we hope you happily go back to your home after your visit with this phrase in mind, “ Gretchensville sure felt like home away from home”.

Step Inside Our Home

Each space thoughtfully designed for comfort and relaxation

Living Room

Imagine sitting down on a comfortable couch, a hand holding a big screen TV remote, and eyes staring at the aromatic baked apple pie on the kitchen counter? That’s the kind of an open concept we designed for Gretchensville. In our living room, you will also find many windows which provide a great deal of natural light and fresh air. And if you’re a CEO of your organization, you can turn this magnificent living room and dining room space into a boardroom and make it comfortable for over fifty people with a big screen on the wall to aid your presentations.

Open Concept Kitchen

Welcome to our spacious modern kitchen with all sorts of sleek appliances. It overlooks a gorgeous kitchen garden with herbs and vegetables that you are welcomed to use anytime in your cooking. We also make sure that our cabinets are well stocked with all sorts of cooking items you may need to make your culinary activity enjoyable. And lastly, to remind you that the kitchen is the heart of the home, we boast a large island surrounded by comfortable bar stools– providing a cozy atmosphere for dining, drinking or conversing while cooking.

Dining Area

Step into a warm and inviting dining space where each meal is more than just food — it’s an experience. Thoughtfully designed with natural textures, ambient lighting, and cozy seating, our dining area offers the perfect setting to enjoy a fresh homemade breakfast or a quiet evening tea. Whether you're starting your day with the aroma of brewed coffee or winding down in the golden glow of sunset, this space invites you to relax, connect, and savor every moment.

Kids Room

We did not leave the little ones behind when we designed The Gretchensville Home. With our snuggy bunk bed in place, and assorted toys nearby, we provide a childproof, good looking and spacious place for the kids. And if needed, we can provide you with a crib for your little baby. The room is also equipped with a small desk that the young stars can use for reading or painting. This room is deliberately located next to our first floor bedroom so that the parents can keep a close eye on their kids.

Deluxe Rooms

This is a 5 bedroom house scattered all over the house. Our usage of the rooms depends on the number and needs of our guests.

These are your standard size rooms with mahogany beds of different sizes, bed stands, good lighting and more.

Each room also has a generous closet with cloth hangers available for you. Some rooms also have bedside tables that are ideal for studying.
